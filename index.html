Welcome

The “Danse Macabre Ball” is an exciting gathering for all those who enjoy and appreciate that which is dark and mysterious. It’s a night of self-expression that connects a world of diverse identities through dancing, fashion, and community. This is a grand opportunity to bring out the unique Goth within you.

Join us for this enchanting and mystifying experience!

Dance

The music includes Post-Punk, Industrial, and Darkwave to be provided by Owen Owington Owensford, a locally loved DJ who also tours nationally, and is the host of The Hanging Garden radio show.

Fashion

Whether Goth, Steampunk, Victorian, Punk, Industrial, Dieselpunk, Catrina, Cosplay, or other style, it's an opportunity to celebrate indiviuality and show off that outfit or costume that expresses the inner self.

Community

We support many non-profit organizations such as Ratical Rodent Rescue and JNW Reptile Resuce

And More!

At Lady Lurid's Lounge, you can relax and mingle with all your friends, old and new.
